Translator passionate about language, communication, and the human connection derived from it.

I offer translation and version services in the language combination English - Portuguese (Brazilian).

Graduated in Law at Faculty of Law of São Bernardo do Campo (FDSBC), translation has been a part of my professional routine since 2012. As a lawyer, I have been responsible for the translation of agreements, legal opinions, and legal texts in general, especially regarding entertainment and intellectual property law, besides documents related to other areas of knowledge including music, cinema, commerce and marketing.

I have also been a teacher of English as a second language for all ages and levels of learning, in group or individual classes, online or in person. My experience began in 2010 and, since then, I have worked with private classes and with language schools, which includes participating in the coordination of one of them.
